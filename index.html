Publications

During the academic year 2019/20, I conducted my master thesis that resulted in my own first scientific paper entitled “Prenatal exposure to Greenspace and Cord Blood Cortisol Levels”, published in the D1 peer-reviewed journal Environment International (Impact factor: 7.94). (Read here)
Under the supervision of Dr. Dadvand, I conducted the analysis of the dataset, literature research and preparation of the manuscript. Additionally, I contributed to a systematic review assessing the impact of urban greenspace on brain development in children, which is currently under review. Beyond my internship, I am working on a scientific paper investigating the effect of greenspace exposure on the development of phobias in schoolchildren.
