Education

Young Researcher with a B.Sc in Biomedical Sciences and a M.Sc in Public Health, currently expanding on my computational and data-analysis skills

Since 2020

Master of Science in Bioinformatics

Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona (Spain)
Last summer, I enrolled in my 2nd master’s program in Bioinformatics at the UPF, Barcelona. The comprehensive curriculum introduces me to state-of-the-art algorithms and computational paradigms. Given the exponential growth of health-related data that accompanied the technological revolution, well-grounded usage of machine learning and data mining became indispensable means to successful research.

2018-2020

Master of Science in Public Health
University of Southern Denmark, Campus Esberg (Denmark)
Thesis: Prenatal greenspace exposure and cord blood cortisol levels: A cross-sectional study in a middle-income country.

The combination of statistics, applied medical theory and socio-political context gave me an overarching insight into today’s burdens of global health and taught me how to plan, monitor and assess a variety of projects.

2015-2018

Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Science – Philipps Universität Marburg (Germany)

The demanding, scientific workload sharpened my analytical skills, selfdiscipline and equipped me with a strong biomedical understanding of the biomedical field.
